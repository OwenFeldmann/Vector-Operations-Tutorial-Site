Vector Subtraction

The difference of two vectors v and u can be found by subtracting corresponding vector elements. 

	v-u = (v1-u1, v2-u2, ... , vn-un)

This method can be extended for use from 1 dimensional vectors to n-dimensional vectors.


Tutorial Use

On the canvas on the left, define your vectors by clicking the starting point, and the the ending point for each vector. After both vectors are defined, clicking again will clear the vectors and define the starting point of the new first vector. The values of the vectors and the result of the vector addition operation are displayed in the canvas once both vectors are defined.
